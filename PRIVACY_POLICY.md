# Privacy Policy for LiveOFF

**Last updated:** 2026-05-28

## Overview

LiveOFF is an offline survival guide application. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

## Data Collection

**LiveOFF does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.** All data is stored locally on your device.

### Data stored locally on your device:
- **Favorites and notes** — saved in a local database on your device
- **Settings and preferences** — stored locally using Android DataStore

### Permissions used:
- **Camera** — used solely to control the device flashlight for Morse code transmission. No photos or videos are taken or stored.
- **Vibration** — used for Morse code transmission via vibration.
- **SMS** — used only when you explicitly trigger the SOS function to send an emergency message to a contact you specify. No SMS is sent without your direct action.
- **Phone calls** — used only when you explicitly trigger the SOS function to call an emergency contact you specify. No calls are made without your direct action.

## Third-Party Services

LiveOFF does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. No data leaves your device.

## Data Retention

All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data.

## Children's Privacy

LiveOFF does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
